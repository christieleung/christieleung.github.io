colours of nostaglia

anecdotes from childhood

yellow

So many of my family pictures were taken on this pale yellow leather couch. While I do not look the happiest in this photo, I remember loving how comfortable it was to sink into and how fun it was to jump on when my mom was not looking.

red

My second birthday cake was heart-shaped and decorated with strawberries and a singular maraschino cherry. I was eyeing that bright red treasure during the entirety of my birthday song and while blowing out the candles, not needing to wish for anything more.

blue

Kindergarten graduation! I was so proud of the glow-in-the-dark beaded necklace I made and how neatly I signed my name on the back of this blue t-shirt. While it was not my best outfit, I did feel great dancing and singing in it.
